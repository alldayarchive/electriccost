📊 Understanding Tiered Pricing (Progressive Tax)

Most countries use a "Tiered Pricing" system. This means the more electricity you use, the higher the rate per kWh becomes.

Example: The first 200 kWh might cost $0.10/kWh, but usage over 200 kWh might jump to $0.20/kWh. This calculator estimates based on a single average rate, so your actual bill could be higher if you exceed certain thresholds.
